Claims to verify or refute:
1. Pair differences: for same-grid replicates with static truth,
   E[(y_a−y_b)²/2 | I] = σ²(I) pointwise; the per-point regression of
   d²/2 on I with IRLS weights 1/pred² is unbiased for σ²(I)=a+b·I (the
   code documents that per-bin aggregation and observed-variance weights
   each bias b low ~10-15% — is the IRLS fix itself unbiased, including
   the χ²₁ heavy tail?).
2. Registration: the pair shift is estimated from the Taylor-leakage
   coefficient, then the pair is aligned by np.interp with BOTH signs
   tried (lowest residual kept). Is the linear-interp noise transmission
   factor rf=(1−f)²+f² applied correctly in pair_var_factor = 1+rf (Var
   of aligned difference)? Any bias from choosing the sign/alignment by
   minimum residual variance (selection on noise)?
3. Drift regression: bases [y′, y″, mean, const] fitted per pair by
   OLS — degrees-of-freedom loss is ~4/n (n≈300); noise absorbed into
   the basis coefficients biases σ² low by O(4/n)? Acceptable/documented?
4. High-pass: d_hp = d − movavg_k(d) with variance correction 1−1/k.
   Exact for white noise with a normalized 'same' convolution at the
   edges? (Edge points are excluded from the samples — enough?) What
   about noise correlated on scales ≥ k (the code claims only
   under-counting there — direction correct?)
5. The χ²₁-median 0.4549 correction is now used ONLY in the diagnostic
   bins — confirm nothing in the FIT path still assumes it.
6. sigma() / weights() / variance() semantics; the second-difference
   fallback σ = 1.4826·MAD(Δ²y)/√6 — is √6 the right factor for the
   second difference of iid noise?
7. Tests: do they discriminate the failure modes above (aggregation
   bias, weight-noise correlation, shift leakage, drift domination)?
   Are the tolerances principled (median-of-seeds pins)?
8. Survey honesty: sub-Poisson b=0.61-0.76 on the clean groups is
   interpreted as sweep-averaged exports, with the nonlinear C 1s groups
   flagged rather than fitted — any overreach in
   docs/autofit/PROGRESS.md's noise section?
